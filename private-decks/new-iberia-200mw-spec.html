Redundancy Stack
Bloom SOFC — N+1
Capacity deployed exceeds demand by at least 1 full unit. Any single unit failure: zero impact on load. Bloom MTBF >95,000 hours.

Zone A (Bloom) and Zone C (BESS) each feed an independent DC bus. Rack PDUs accept both feeds. Complete bus failure: secondary bus holds full load.

Bloom restart or fuel supply interruption: BESS maintains full facility load. DC-coupled = instantaneous switchover, no transfer time.
