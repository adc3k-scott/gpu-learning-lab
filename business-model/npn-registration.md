# NVIDIA Partner Network (NPN) Registration — Action Checklist

## Status: NOT STARTED (Priority #1 — Do Today)

## What It Is
NPN is NVIDIA's partner portal. Registration is free and takes ~5 minutes. It's the gateway to:
- DGX-Ready Managed Services certification
- NVIDIA Cloud Partner (NCP) certification
- Hardware allocation and pricing access
- Co-marketing and lead generation
- Technical training and enablement

## Registration Steps

### Step 1: Create NVIDIA Account
- URL: https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/account/
- Use scott@adc3k.com (business email, NOT personal)
- Company: Advantage Design & Construction

### Step 2: Register as Partner
- URL: https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/about-nvidia/partners/self-registration/
- Partner type: **Cloud Service Provider** (neocloud = CSP in NVIDIA's taxonomy)

### Step 3: Select Competencies
- **Compute** — primary (AI factory / GPU infrastructure)
- **DGX AI Compute Systems** — signals serious hardware buyer
- **Networking** — InfiniBand / Spectrum (DSX reference design)

### Step 4: Company Profile
- Industry: Cloud / Data Center Infrastructure
- Size: Startup (1-10)
- Region: North America
- Description: "Louisiana-based AI factory operator building DSX-compliant GPU compute facilities. NCA-AIIO certified. Targeting NVIDIA Cloud Partner (NCP) certification."

### Step 5: Submit and Wait
- Approval timeline: 2-6 weeks
- NVIDIA assigns a Partner Development Manager (PDM)
- PDM is your primary NVIDIA contact going forward

## What Happens After Approval
1. **Partner Portal access** — pricing, deal registration, technical resources
2. **PDM assigned** — this person becomes the relationship owner
3. **Training access** — NVIDIA Deep Learning Institute (DLI) courses
4. **Co-marketing eligibility** — NVIDIA logo usage, joint press
