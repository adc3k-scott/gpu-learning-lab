The ADC 3K Pod

A manufactured compute unit that drops into any metal structure. No HVAC. No raised floor. No data center build-out required.

01
Full Immersion Cooling
EC-110 dielectric fluid. Every server submerged. No air cooling — works in any warehouse, cannery, or industrial structure without modification.
02
40-ft ISO Container
Standard shipping container form factor. Drop in by crane, connect power and network, commission remotely from MARLIE I. No local construction crew needed long-term.
03
Remote Operations
All pods managed from Mission Control NOC at MARLIE I. Infrastructure agents monitor GPU health, thermals, power, and network fabric in real time.
04
480V 3-Phase + N+1 Backup
Industrial power input. Generator backup standard. Each pod is a self-contained, fault-tolerant compute unit.
