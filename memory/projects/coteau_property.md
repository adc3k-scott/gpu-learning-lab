## 8. Utilities & Infrastructure

### Water
- **Waterworks District No. 3** — 4104 Coteau Road (Hwy 88), New Iberia, LA 70560. Open M-F 8:30a-4:00p. Serves the Coteau area.

### Electricity
- **SLEMCO** (Southwest Louisiana Electric Membership Corporation) — likely provider for rural Iberia Parish/Coteau area. Major co-op serving Acadiana.
- **Cleco** — alternative provider in some parts of the region.
- Iberia Parish is also served by Entergy in some areas.

### Natural Gas
- Louisiana is rich in natural gas infrastructure. Major pipelines cross the region.
- **Kinder Morgan Louisiana Pipeline (KMLP)** originates at Columbia Gulf Transmission in Evangeline Parish (nearby).
- **Southern Natural Gas** — 6,900-mile system extending from LA supply basins.
- **Delta Utilities** (formerly CenterPoint Energy Louisiana) — regulated gas utility.
- Specific provider for Coteau area: check GetGasLA.com or contact Iberia Parish directly.

### Zoning
- Coteau is unincorporated Iberia Parish — zoning governed by parish ordinances.
- Iberia Parish has a permit office for development.
- Louisiana LED Certified Industrial Sites require 25+ contiguous buildable acres.
- Parish zoning typically includes agricultural (A), residential (R), commercial (C), light industrial (M1), heavy industrial (M2).
- **Unincorporated areas in rural Louisiana often have minimal or no zoning restrictions** — this could be advantageous for AI factory development.
