First remote deployment site for ADC 3K containerized pods.

NOT a building-based compute facility — ADC 3K pods drop in as manufactured units.

## Why Trappeys Works for ADC 3K
- Metal warehouse structure = immersion-cooled pods need no HVAC modifications
- Existing site infrastructure (power, gas stubs, road access)
- River frontage (Vermilion River) — potential supplemental heat rejection
- Industrial zoning — no rezoning needed
- Owner has history with the site

## ADC 3K Pod Deployment Here
- Cooling: Immersion (dielectric fluid) — no air conditioning, no CDU, no raised floor
- Each pod: 40-ft ISO container dropped into warehouse bay
- Heat rejection: Exterior dry cooler or river HX (TBD)
- Power: 480V 3-phase per pod + N+1 generator backup
