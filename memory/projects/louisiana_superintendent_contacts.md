## NOTES

1. **LASSA directory caveat**: Some emails on the LASSA member-districts page may be for assistant/executive secretary rather than the superintendent directly. A few appeared mismatched (e.g., Allen Parish listed Brad Soileau but showed michael.doucet@ email). Verify before mass send.
2. **Three districts with no email found**: Caddo Parish (Keith Burton), Ascension Parish (Dr. Edith Walker), Caldwell Parish (Nicki McCann), East Carroll Parish (Dr. Meagan Brown). Phone numbers provided -- recommend a quick call.
3. **Email format patterns by district**: Most follow firstname.lastname@ or flastname@ or first initial + last@. Can be used to educated-guess missing ones but DO NOT send without verification.
4. **Total districts covered**: 69 (64 parish + 5 city/special: Monroe City, Bogalusa City, City of Baker, Central Community, Zachary Community).
5. **LDOE contact for digital/STEM**: DigitalLearning@la.gov and STEM@la.gov are team inboxes -- good for initial outreach.
