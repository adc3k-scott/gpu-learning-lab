# Client Referral Program

**Every installed client becomes a sales channel. The trades refer each other constantly — plumber tells the electrician, electrician tells the HVAC guy, HVAC guy tells the GC. We built the referral program around that reality.**

---

## How It Works

### For the Referrer (Existing Client)
- **1 month free subscription** for every successful referral that completes install and stays active 30+ days.
- No limit. Refer 5 businesses, get 5 months free.
- Stacks — if a client refers 12 businesses in a year, they pay for zero months.

### For the Referred Business (New Client)
- **50% off install fee** — lowers the barrier to say yes.
- Full subscription pricing (no discount on monthly — the install discount is the hook).

### For the Installer
- **10% of first year subscription** from the referred client — paid monthly, on top of normal commission.
- This is bonus money. The installer still gets their normal 30% install commission + 5% recurring override.
- Example: Referred client pays $500/mo. Installer gets an extra $50/mo for 12 months = $600 bonus on top of everything else.

## Referral Code System

Every active client gets a **unique referral code** at install time.

```
Format: [VERTICAL]-[CLIENTID]-[INSTALLER]
Example: PLUMB-0047-JT
```

- Printed on referral cards left at the business.
- Tracked in the AI Advantage dashboard.
- Installer sees which of their clients are referring and gets credit automatically.

## Referral Cards

**Leave 5-10 referral cards at every install.** Non-negotiable.

Cards include:
- Client's unique referral code
- "50% off your install" headline
- QR code linking to ai-advantage.info with referral code pre-filled
- Ally chatbot picks up the lead from there

## The Sweet Spot: Trades Refer Trades

This is where the referral program prints money.

```
Plumber installs AI Advantage
  → Refers electrician (they share jobs daily)
    → Electrician refers HVAC company
      → HVAC refers the GC they all work under
        → GC refers property manager who hires all of them
          → Property manager refers 3 more trades
```

One field services install can cascade into 5-10 referrals within 90 days because these businesses **already talk to each other every day.**

Same pattern in other clusters:
- **Medical cluster**: Dentist → orthodontist → oral surgeon → pediatrician
- **Legal cluster**: Family law → real estate attorney → CPA → insurance agent
- **Retail cluster**: Clothing store → shoe store → gift shop (same strip mall)

## Tracking and Metrics

### Dashboard Tracking
- Every referral code scanned or entered is logged.
- Installer dashboard shows: referrals generated per client, conversion rate, pending vs. completed.
- Client portal shows their referral count and free months earned.

### Target Metrics
| Metric | Target | Timeline |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Referral rate (% of clients who refer at least 1) | 30% | By month 6 |
| Referrals per referring client | 2-3 | First year average |
| Referral conversion rate (referred lead → install) | 50%+ | Ongoing |
| % of new clients from referrals | 30% | By month 6 |

### Why 30% Referral-Sourced is the Goal
- Referral clients have **zero acquisition cost** (no ads, no cold outreach).
- Referral clients close faster (warm intro, social proof already done).
- Referral clients churn less (they saw it working at another business first).
- Every referral client that refers someone else creates a **compound growth loop**.

## Referral Program Rules

1. **Referral must complete install and stay active 30 days** before referrer gets free month.
2. **Free months apply to current subscription tier** — no gaming by downgrading.
3. **Installer referral bonus (10% of first year)** is separate from recurring override. Both apply.
4. **Self-referrals don't count.** Same owner, different business = fine. Same business, same owner = no.
5. **No limit on referrals.** A client who refers 20 businesses gets 20 free months. That client is a hero — treat them like one.
