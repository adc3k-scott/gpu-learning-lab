## The 5-Step Process

### Step 1: Discovery Call (15 minutes)
We learn about your business. What do you do every day? What takes too long? What falls through the cracks? We match you to one of our 12 industry playbooks — pre-built AI systems designed specifically for businesses like yours.

**Client hears:** "We've already built this for your industry. We're not experimenting on you."

---

### Step 2: We Show Up (Install Day — 2-3 hours)
A trained AI Advantage installer comes to your location with everything you need. We set up the hardware (if needed), install your AI agent, configure it for your specific business, and lock it down with enterprise-grade security.

Your AI agent runs in a **secure sandbox** — it can only access the tools and services your business actually uses. Nothing else. No surprises.

**Client hears:** "It's like hiring a new employee who already knows your industry, works 24/7, and can't access anything they're not supposed to."

---

### Step 3: Training (30-45 minutes, same visit)
Before we leave, we train your team. We show everyone how to talk to the agent, what it can do, and what to expect. No tech skills needed — if you can send a text message, you can use this.

**Client hears:** "Your staff will be using it before we walk out the door."

---

### Step 4: We Monitor, You Work
After install, we monitor your AI agent remotely. If it tries to connect to something new, we review it and approve or block it. If something needs updating, we handle it. You don't touch anything technical — ever.

**Client hears:** "We manage it like a security company manages your alarm system. You just use it."

---

### Step 5: It Gets Smarter
As your business uses the agent, it learns your patterns. We update models, add capabilities, and optimize performance — all remotely, all included in your subscription. No disruption. No downtime.

**Client hears:** "It keeps getting better and you don't have to do anything."

## FAQ (What Clients Actually Ask)

**"What if my internet goes down?"**
If you're on our DGX Spark plan, the AI keeps running — it's on your local device. Cloud-only plans need internet, but we set up automatic failover messaging so your customers still get a response.

**"Can the AI access my bank account / email / personal stuff?"**
No. The sandbox blocks everything except the specific business tools we configure together. If the AI tries to reach anything we didn't approve, it gets blocked instantly.

**"Who else can see my data?"**
Nobody. We monitor the agent's behavior (what it's connecting to), not your business data. On DGX Spark, your data stays on your hardware entirely.

**"What happens if I cancel?"**
We remove the agent and return your systems to how they were. Your data stays yours. No lock-in, no hostage situation.

**"Is this like ChatGPT?"**
ChatGPT is a general chatbot on a website. This is a dedicated AI agent configured specifically for your industry, running on secure hardware, monitored by professionals, and integrated into your actual business tools. It's the difference between googling a legal question and hiring a paralegal.
