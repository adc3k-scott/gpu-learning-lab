Questions & Answers

Everything you want to know. No runaround.

We get these questions every day. Here are straight answers. If you don't see yours, ask ROXY — our AI assistant — in the chat bubble below.

What exactly does AI Advantage do?

+

We install a dedicated AI agent at your business, configured specifically for your industry. It can answer phones, manage schedules, track inventory, draft documents, handle customer follow-ups — whatever your business needs. We set it up, train your team, and manage it remotely. You just use it.

Is this like ChatGPT?

+

ChatGPT is a general chatbot on a website. This is a dedicated AI agent built for your specific industry, running on secure hardware, monitored by professionals, and integrated into your actual business tools. It's the difference between googling a legal question and hiring a paralegal.

How long does setup take?

+

One visit. 2-3 hours. Our installer shows up with everything, sets up the hardware and software, configures it for your business, trains your team in 30 minutes, and verifies everything is running before they leave. You're using it the same day.

Do I need to be tech-savvy to use this?

+

If you can send a text message, you can use this. You talk to your AI agent in plain English — through a chat window, text message, or even a phone call. No menus, no training manuals, no learning curve. We train your whole team in 30 minutes before we leave.

What industries do you work with?

+

We have pre-built playbooks for 12 industries: law firms, medical/dental, field services (plumbing, HVAC, electrical), restaurants, auto shops, real estate, construction, property management, retail, accounting/CPA, salons/barbers, and insurance agencies. If your industry isn't listed, ask us — we can likely build a custom playbook.

What equipment do I need?

+

It depends on your plan. Cloud Only ($0 hardware) — just your existing computer and internet. Starter Kit ($1,200) — we bring a tablet, router, and scanner. Mac Mini ($1,699) — a small silent device that sits on your desk. DGX Spark ($5,499) — NVIDIA's AI supercomputer for compliance-heavy industries. Most businesses start with Cloud Only or Mac Mini.

Do I need to buy new computers?

+

No. Our Cloud Only tier works with whatever you already have — laptop, desktop, tablet, even your phone. If your equipment is really outdated, our Starter Kit ($1,200) includes everything you'd need. But most businesses can start with what they've got.

What's the Mac Mini option and why would I want it?

+

The Mac Mini is a small, silent computer (7 inches square) that sits on a desk or shelf. It runs your AI agent locally — meaning your data never leaves your building. It uses about 40 watts (less than a light bulb), requires zero maintenance, and gives you the fastest response times. It's our most popular option for businesses that care about privacy.

What internet speed do I need?

+

For Cloud Only plans, any stable internet connection works — even basic broadband. For Mac Mini and DGX Spark plans, the AI runs locally on your device, so internet speed barely matters. We bring a backup hotspot to every install just in case your connection has issues.

What if my internet goes down?

+

If you're on a Mac Mini or DGX Spark plan, your AI agent keeps running — it's on your local device and doesn't need internet to think. Cloud-only plans need internet, but we set up automatic failover messaging so your customers still get a response even when you're offline.

Where does the hardware actually go?

+

Wherever you want. The Mac Mini is 7 inches square — it fits on a desk, shelf, or in a closet. The DGX Spark is a bit larger but still desktop-sized. We connect it to your existing network with one ethernet cable, plug it in, and that's it. No server room needed. No special wiring.

Can the AI see my personal files, email, or bank account?

+

No. Your AI agent runs inside an isolated sandbox — think of it as a locked room. It can only see files in its own workspace. It cannot access your email, personal files, bank accounts, or anything else on your computer or network. We configure exactly what it can touch, together with you, during install.

Can you see my business data?

+

We monitor your agent's behavior — what it's connecting to, whether it's running properly, if anything looks unusual. We do not read your business data. On Mac Mini and DGX Spark plans, your data stays entirely on your hardware in your building. We can't see it even if we wanted to.

Is this HIPAA compliant? What about law firms?

+

Yes. Medical and legal clients get compliance-grade security: strict filesystem isolation (patient data / case files never leave the device), full audit logging of every agent action, blocked access to cloud storage and email, and on-site hardware so nothing travels over the internet. We recommend DGX Spark for all HIPAA and attorney-privilege environments.

What if the AI tries to do something it shouldn't?

+

Every network connection your agent makes is checked against an approved list. If it tries to reach a website or service we didn't approve, it gets blocked automatically and our team is notified immediately. We review every blocked request and take action if anything looks wrong. You're covered 24/7.

What does it actually cost per month?

+

Three plans: Basic at $199/month (core AI features, 1-2 users, email support), Pro at $699/month (all features, AI phone answering, 5 users, phone support — our most popular), and Enterprise at $1,299/month (unlimited users, multi-location, custom workflows, dedicated account manager). Hardware is a separate one-time purchase. No long-term contracts.

Are there any hidden fees or long-term contracts?

+

No and no. Your monthly subscription covers everything — the agent, security monitoring, updates, support, and model upgrades. Hardware is a one-time cost. No setup fees, no cancellation penalties, no surprise charges. If you cancel, we remove the agent and you keep your data. That's it.

Is the hardware a rental or do I own it?

+

You own it. The Mac Mini, DGX Spark, Starter Kit — whatever you buy is yours. If you cancel your subscription, you keep the hardware. We remove our software, and the device is yours to repurpose however you want.

What happens after install? Do I manage this myself?

+

You never manage anything technical. After install, we monitor your agent remotely 24/7. If something needs updating, we handle it. If the AI needs a model upgrade, we push it — no disruption, no downtime. You just use the agent. Think of it like a managed alarm system — you arm it and go to bed, we watch the cameras.

What if something breaks or the AI stops working?

+

We catch most issues before you even notice — our monitoring detects problems in real time. If something does go down, Pro and Enterprise plans include phone support for immediate help. Basic plan has email support with fast response times. In most cases, we can fix issues remotely without visiting your location.

What if I want to cancel?

+

Cancel anytime — no penalties, no guilt trip. We remove the AI agent, return your systems to how they were, and your data stays yours. Hardware you purchased is yours to keep. We don't do lock-in. If we're not earning your business every month, that's on us.
