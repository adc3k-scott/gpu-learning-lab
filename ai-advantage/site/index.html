Security

Your data stays yours. Period.

Every AI agent runs inside a locked-down sandbox powered by NVIDIA NemoClaw. It can only access what we approve together. Nothing else gets through.

Sandboxed Execution

Your agent runs in an isolated container. It cannot see anything outside its workspace — not your email, not your personal files, not your bank. Hardware-enforced isolation.

Network Policy

Only approved connections allowed. If the agent tries to reach a website we didn't approve, it gets blocked instantly and our team is notified. You control the list.

Monitored 24/7

Our team monitors every agent remotely. We handle security updates, policy changes, and model upgrades. You never touch anything technical.

Your Files, Your Pace

Load files when you're ready. We set up the folders — you decide what goes in them. Your agent can help organize, find duplicates, and clean up storage.

On-Site Option

Choose our Mac Mini or DGX Spark plans and your data never leaves your building. The AI runs entirely on hardware sitting in your office.

HIPAA / Legal Ready
