What is this course?

This is a free, self-paced online course from NVIDIA that covers the fundamentals of AI infrastructure — GPUs, data centers, cloud computing, software ecosystems, and compute platforms. No prior technical background required. You'll finish with a genuine NVIDIA certificate and a solid foundation in how AI systems actually work.

1
Go to the NVIDIA Learning Platform

Open any web browser and navigate to:

learn.nvidia.com

This is NVIDIA's official training hub. You can also find it by going to nvidia.com → Training → Self-Paced Courses, or just Google "NVIDIA AI Infrastructure Fundamentals".

Note: Chrome or Edge browser gives the smoothest video playback experience. The site works on mobile but a laptop or desktop is recommended.
2
Create a Free NVIDIA Account

You need a free NVIDIA account to enroll. Here's exactly how to create one:

  1. On the NVIDIA learning platform, click the "Sign In" or "Join" button in the top-right corner.
  2. Click "Create Account" — you'll be taken to NVIDIA's account registration page at learn.nvidia.com/join.
  3. Enter your email address and click Continue.
  4. Fill in your first name, last name, and create a password (at least 8 characters, include a number or symbol).
  5. Check your email for a verification message from NVIDIA. Click the link inside to confirm your account. (Check spam if you don't see it within a few minutes.)
  6. Once verified, sign back in with your email and password — you're in.
Already have an NVIDIA account? If you've ever registered on nvidia.com, GeForce Experience, or any other NVIDIA product, use those same credentials — no need to create a new account.
3
Find the Course

Once signed in, find the course using any of these methods:

Option A — Search
  1. Click the search icon or "Find Training" in the top navigation.
  2. Type: AI Infrastructure and Operations Fundamentals
  3. Click the course when it appears in results.
Option B — Browse by Topic
  1. Click "Training By Topic" in the top navigation.
  2. Select "AI and Data Science" or "AI Infrastructure".
  3. Scroll to find AI Infrastructure and Operations Fundamentals.
Option C — Direct Link

Go straight to the NVIDIA training catalog: nvidia.com/en-us/training

4
Enroll in the Course
  1. On the course page, you'll see the title, description, and a list of all modules.
  2. Click the "Enroll" or "Start Course" button. No payment required — it's free.
  3. The course is automatically added to your learning dashboard.
  4. Click "Start" to begin Module 1.
If you see a cost listed: Make sure you're signed into your account — free courses require a free login to unlock at no charge.
5

6
Get Your Certificate
  1. Complete all video modules and pass all knowledge check quizzes.
  2. After finishing the final section, your certificate is generated automatically.
  3. Go to "My Learning" in the top navigation and find the completed course.
  4. Click "View Certificate" or "Download Certificate" to save or print it.
  5. You can also share it directly to LinkedIn straight from the certificate page.
Your certificate includes your name, the course title, completion date, and is officially issued by NVIDIA — a legitimate, recognized credential in the AI industry.
