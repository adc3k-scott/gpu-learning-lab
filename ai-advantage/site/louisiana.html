01 — Power Economics
40 Miles from Henry Hub. That Changes Everything.

Every natural gas contract in North America prices relative to Henry Hub in Erath, Louisiana — the global benchmark since 1990. Operators in Virginia or Texas pay Henry Hub price plus a basis differential for transport. We pay the benchmark price itself. Our basis differential is near zero.

Henry Hub — Erath, Louisiana

The pricing benchmark for every gas molecule in North America.

Interconnects 13 pipelines — 9 interstate, 4 intrastate — with 1.8 billion cubic feet/day throughput. Direct connections to salt dome storage caverns. The most interconnected gas hub on the planet. Global LNG contracts now index to Henry Hub.

What "At The Source" Means

Everyone else pays transport. We don't.

Gas price anywhere = Henry Hub spot + basis differential + pipeline tariffs. Northeast operators pay $0.50–$2.00+/MMBtu on top of Henry Hub just for transport. South Louisiana basis differential: near zero or negative. ADC sites sit on existing Atmos Energy infrastructure. Gas is already there.

The Math — Why This Matters at Scale

A 2 MW AI facility running 24/7 consumes ~17,500 MWh per year. At $0.04/kWh (Henry Hub genset), that's $700K/year in energy cost. The same facility in Virginia at $0.11/kWh pays $1.93M/year. That's a $1.2M annual energy advantage — recurring, compounding, and widening as grid rates climb. And unlike grid-dependent operators facing 4+ year interconnection queues, on-site natural gas generation bypasses the grid queue entirely.

On-Site Power Stack
Bloom SOFC

Primary Generation

Bloom SOFC converts natural gas to 800V DC direct. 54% electrical efficiency. No inverter, no rectifier, no AC anywhere. Atmos Energy supply at Henry Hub pricing — no utility middleman, no rate increases, no demand charges.

Cat G3520H

Field & Bridge Power

Natural gas reciprocating genset (2,507 kW continuous) paired with an Eaton AC-DC rectifier skid that feeds the same 800V DC bus. Bridges Bloom allocation gaps. N+1 at any site. No diesel — same Atmos/Henry Hub gas supply.

First Solar

Daytime Offset

Rooftop solar panels manufactured in New Iberia, Louisiana — 30 miles from our sites. Reduces fuel burn during peak daylight hours. Supports ESG reporting for enterprise customers. DC-direct integration at 97% efficiency.

LFP BESS

DC-Coupled Bridge

DC-coupled LFP battery (4-hour minimum, Hitachi AMPS PCS) provides instant failover during any source transition. Zero perceived downtime to hosted workloads. Millisecond-class response.

02 — Government Incentive Stack
Federal, State, and Local — Stacked in Our Favor.

Louisiana has the most AI-infrastructure-friendly incentive environment in the country. These aren't theoretical — they are active programs with proven precedent, and ADC qualifies for multiple layers simultaneously. These programs stack: the same project can access federal, state, and local incentives concurrently.

Federal Programs
OBBBA — Signed July 4, 2025

100% Bonus Depreciation

Full immediate expensing of all GPU servers, networking hardware, cooling systems, and tangible property with class life of 20 years or less. Permanent. No application required. Retroactive to property acquired after January 19, 2025. First time Congress has made 100% bonus depreciation permanent.

Opportunity Zones 2.0

Tax-Free Capital Gains

MARLIE 1 sits in a designated Opportunity Zone tract on the Evangeline Thruway corridor. Capital gains invested through Qualified Opportunity Funds are tax-deferred; gains held 10+ years are tax-free. OBBBA made this permanent and added a 30% rural step-up bonus.

New Markets Tax Credits

39% Credit Over 7 Years

Equity investments channeled through Community Development Entities into low-income census tracts earn a 39% federal tax credit claimed over 7 years. OBBBA permanently extended the program with a 20% increase in rural allocations. OZ tracts overlap — double-stack eligible.

IRA Solar + Battery ITC

30% Investment Tax Credit

30% federal credit on solar and battery storage installations. Construction must begin before July 4, 2026 — OBBBA phases out clean energy credits after that date. Narrow window. ADC's solar/battery installations are positioned to qualify.

CHIPS Act

Domestic AI Infrastructure

$50 billion in federal funding for domestic semiconductor and AI infrastructure. ADC's 100% US hardware supply chain (NVIDIA Texas manufacturing, Vertiv Ohio cooling) positions it for FEOC-clean federal procurement channels and EDA Tech Hub designation.

BEAD Broadband

$1.355 Billion to Louisiana

Louisiana received $1.355B in BEAD broadband funding — 8th largest allocation in the nation. First state approved (November 2025). ADC as an anchor fiber customer strengthens LUS Fiber's case for BEAD-funded backhaul expansion along the Evangeline corridor.

State of Louisiana
Quality Jobs Rebate

6% Payroll Rebate — 10 Years

Up to 6% rebate on annual gross payroll for 10 years, plus a 1.5% project facility expense rebate on total capital investment. Software is an eligible industry. Only 5 new jobs required for companies with 50 or fewer employees. Active program through LED.

Act 730 — Digital Infrastructure

20-Year Tax Exemption

State and local sales/use tax exemption on AI factory equipment — servers, cooling, cabling, construction materials, modular units. 20-year initial term, 10-year renewal. Enacted 2024 (HB 827). Requires $200M capital investment and 50 permanent jobs at full buildout.

Restoration Tax Abatement

10-Year Property Tax Abatement

Up to 10-year abatement of property taxes on renovations to existing commercial structures in development districts or Opportunity Zones. Directly applicable to the Trappeys Cannery conversion. File before construction starts.

Enterprise Zone

$3,500/Job + Sales Tax Rebate

One-time credit of $3,500 per net new job plus rebate of state sales/use taxes on qualifying equipment OR a 1.5% refundable investment tax credit. Minimum 5 permanent net new jobs within 24 months. 50% of hires from targeted groups.

Angel Investor Tax Credit

25–35% Credit on Investment

25% tax credit on investments in LED-certified Louisiana businesses. Increased to 35% in qualifying parishes. Up to $720K per business per year. Eligible sectors include technology and energy. Expires June 30, 2026 — first-come, first-served.

R&D Tax Credit

Up to 30% on R&D Spend

30% credit for companies with fewer than 50 employees on qualified R&D expenditures in Louisiana. Also 30% on SBIR/STTR grants. $12M annual statewide cap. FY 2025-2026 credits are fully issued — next allocation opens July 1, 2026.

ITEP — Industrial Tax Exemption

Up to $8–16M in Tax Savings

Louisiana's Industrial Tax Exemption Program exempts 80% of ad valorem taxes on qualifying manufacturing and industrial property for 10 years. ADC is pre-qualifying with LED as an emerging technology industry. Estimated project savings: $8–16M. Requires Governor's Office of Economic Development approval. File before construction starts.

Local & Parish Level
PILOT Agreements

10–40 Year Tax Reduction

Payment-in-lieu-of-taxes through parish Industrial Development Boards. Property titled to the IDB becomes exempt from ad valorem taxes. Precedent set: Hut 8 / West Feliciana Parish — 30-year PILOT, $2.5B AI facility, $90M/year parish revenue.

LEDA

Site Selection + Incentive Access

Lafayette Economic Development Authority provides site selection assistance, tax incentive counseling, and workforce development advisory. LEDA serves as the local conduit for state programs (Quality Jobs, ITEP, Enterprise Zone) and facilitates IDB/PILOT structures.

IDB Revenue Bonds

Tax-Exempt Financing

Tax-exempt revenue bonds issued through Lafayette Parish IDB. Louisiana Public Facilities Authority has issued over $12.65 billion in private activity bonds. 90–120 day process. Requires State Bond Commission and LED approval.

Why This Stack Matters

These programs stack. An AI factory in a Louisiana Opportunity Zone can simultaneously access 100% bonus depreciation (federal), Opportunity Zone tax-free gains (federal), New Markets Tax Credits (federal), Quality Jobs payroll rebates (state), ITEP property tax exemption (state), PILOT property tax reduction (local), and IDB tax-exempt financing (local) — all on the same project. No other state in the country offers this combination at this cost of power.
