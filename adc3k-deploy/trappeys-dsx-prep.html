Tool 2 — DSX Boost

Power Optimization — Solar-Constrained Site

DSX Boost delivers 93% throughput at 85% power. On a site where every watt comes from First Solar panels or metered grid, this is the single biggest revenue multiplier.

Without DSX Boost

Baseline
Phase 3 IT Load: 520 kW
GPU Throughput: 100% baseline
Tokens/sec (4 racks): ~2.8B tokens/sec
Revenue at $0.20/M tokens: ~$48K/day
Power cost (solar+grid): ~$0.06/kWh
Daily power cost: ~$750

With DSX Boost

Optimized
Phase 3 IT Load: 442 kW (85%)
GPU Throughput: 93% (only 7% loss)
Tokens/sec (4 racks): ~2.6B tokens/sec
Revenue at $0.20/M tokens: ~$44.6K/day
Power cost: ~$637/day
Net: $113/day saved, or fit MORE racks in same envelope

The Real Play

More Racks Same Power
520 kW power budget stays the same
With Boost: each rack draws 110 kW (not 130)
Fit ~4.7 racks instead of 4
Translation: 5th rack feasible in Phase 3
That's 360 GPUs instead of 288
+25% more compute, same power bill
Boost pays for itself instantly
DSX Boost activates at the GPU firmware level. It ships with NVL72 racks. No additional hardware purchase. You configure it via Base Command Manager. The optimization is automatic once enabled — Max-Q power profiles on Blackwell GPUs.
