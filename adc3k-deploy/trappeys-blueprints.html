E-001 Single-Line Diagram (Per Block)
Expected path: trappeys/blueprints/electrical-sld.svg
Per-block single-line. Fuel: ATMOS natural gas on-site. Generation: 4 × Cat CG260-16 natural gas gensets at 4,000 ekW each (N+1, design anchor 61.5% loading), paralleled at 13.8 kV via Cat ECS isochronous sharing. MV bus: arc-resistant switchgear with 87B bus differential and three-stage UFLS. Transformer: 13.8 kV Δ → 480 Y/277 V, ~15 MVA, dry-type or cast-resin, 87T overall differential. LV: 480 VAC switchboard with LSIG main trip unit and 50G residual ground sensing. DER: block-level LFP BESS (~3.6 MWh working per block) AC-coupled through a Hitachi AMPS PCS SiC bidirectional inverter, and the 2.05 MW First Solar rooftop array (B1/B2) AC-coupled through a grid-forming PV inverter. Cassette feeds: four 480 VAC feeds per block to an Eaton Magnum DS 6,000 A disconnect at each cassette ECP. The 800 VDC rail exists only inside each cassette, where five Delta 660 kW in-row power racks rectify to a cassette-internal 800 VDC busway that feeds nine compute racks plus one control rack. No campus MV ring. No inter-block tie day one. No LUS grid interconnect day one.

Cooling

C-001: Cooling Flow Schematic (GPU & CHP)

Two Independent Paths
SVG Not Loaded
C-001 Cooling Flow Schematic
Expected path: trappeys/blueprints/cooling-schematic.svg
Two independent heat paths. GPU path (per cassette): NVIDIA NVL72/CPX compute rack liquid cold-plates deliver heat to a CoolIT CHx2000 external CDU skid mounted adjacent to the cassette; the CDU closes a PG25 internal loop and exchanges heat through a facility plate heat exchanger (PHX-001) at the cassette boundary. Staubli UQD-25 quick-disconnects at the rack interface; GPU supply ≤45°C, return ~50–55°C. Facility warm-water header (open, raw water) routes through PHX-001 to the cooling tower field in the infrastructure yard — wet mechanical draft working basis, sized for Lafayette's 28°C ASHRAE 0.4% design wet-bulb. Stage 1 total GPU heat rejection ~72.9 MW (~145.7 MW Full Build). CHP path (genset waste heat, independent): Cat CG260-16 exhaust is split — a 125 kW slip-stream per cassette drives Munters DSS Pro desiccant regeneration (5.5 MW Stage 1 desiccant duty, 11 MW Full Build), remainder to stack. Jacket water (~58 MW Stage 1) rejects through dedicated air-cooled radiators on the rear slab. No heat recovery unit. No absorption chiller. No chilled-water plant. The Vermilion River is not a thermal sink.
Power Distribution

P-001: Power Distribution — 11 Blocks, Stage 1

Behind-the-Meter Permanent Island
SVG Not Loaded
P-001 Power Distribution
Expected path: trappeys/blueprints/power-distribution.svg
Stage 1 campus distribution: 11 replicated blocks, behind-the-meter permanent island. Each block is electrically self-contained. Per block: 4 × Cat CG260-16 gensets paralleled at 13.8 kV → ~15 MVA step-down transformer → 480 VAC switchboard (single convergence point for generation, storage, solar, cooling, cassette feeds) → 4 cassette primary feeds. Stage 1 total generation capacity at 61.5% anchor loading: 108.24 MW — positive margin of ~2 to 4 MW over total campus load (~104–106 MW working estimate). BESS (LFP chemistry, ~3.6 MWh working per block, 39.6 MWh Stage 1 total) AC-coupled through Hitachi AMPS PCS SiC bidirectional inverter — always active as stabilizer, not standby. Solar (2.05 MW First Solar Series 7 CdTe on B1/B2 rooftops) AC-coupled through grid-forming PV inverter. Protection coordinates inside each block: 87G generator differential per unit; 87B bus differential on the 13.8 kV MV bus; 87T transformer differential; LSIG 480 VAC main trip unit; three-stage UFLS at the MV inlet shedding cassette lanes in priority order. No campus-level MV ring, no inter-block tie day one; each block's blast radius is contained. LUS Pinhook Substation is spatially allocated as future optionality only; day-one operation is permanent island mode. Sub-100 ms GPU transients are handled inside the cassette by rack-integrated BBU and Power Capacitance Shelf.
Solar

L-001: Solar Rooftop Layout (B1 + B2)

2.05 MW · 3,731 Panels
SVG Not Loaded
L-001 Solar Rooftop Layout
Expected path: trappeys/blueprints/solar-layout.svg
First Solar Series 7 CdTe thin-film rooftop array across Buildings B1 and B2 (the two historic NOC / partner hub buildings). 3,731 × FS-7550A-TR1 panels, 550 W each, 2.05 MW DC nameplate. 5-panel strings at 1,500 VDC (NEC LVDC ceiling); MPP ranges ~891 V (summer high-cell-temp) to ~1,013 V (winter cool-cell), within standard 1,500 VDC-rated PV inverter MPPT range. 746 complete strings with disconnects per combiner. Grid-forming PV inverter ties to block 480 VAC bus (B1/B2 tied to nearest block switchboard); anti-islanding protection intentionally disabled because the facility is the electrical island. First Solar domestic content qualifies the array for the 40% Federal Solar ITC (30% base + 10% domestic content adder) — First Solar manufactures in Perrysburg, Ohio and New Iberia, Louisiana (30 miles from site). Array at design basis provides ~2% of Stage 1 campus load under peak irradiance; dispatched via AMCL L3 as solar recapture against BESS charge state.
