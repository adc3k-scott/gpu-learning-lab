Ragin' Cajun Compute Campus — Engineering Blueprints

Trappeys Cannery, Lafayette, LA | 5-Sheet MVP Design Intent Package | NOT FOR CONSTRUCTION
Electrical

E-001: Electrical Single-Line Diagram

800V DC Native
ATMOS natural gas → Bloom Energy SOFC (solid oxide fuel cell) — DC-direct output, no AC stage, no combustion, no spinning parts. DC-DC converter → 800V DC distribution bus (OCP Stage 1d, LVDC). Solar collection layer: 1500V DC strings (NEC LVDC ceiling) → MPPT DC-DC → battery bank → 800V bus. Supercapacitors on bus absorb AI training load spikes. ABB SACE Infinitus solid-state DC breakers for arc-free protection. Delta 660 kW Power Rack (6x 110 kW shelves, 480 kW embedded BBU) with e-Fuse SiC switch (<3 μs fault cutoff) and 90 kW DC/DC shelf (800V to 50V, 98.5%, NVIDIA MGX). Delta 140 kW In-Rack CDU (4RU, NVL72 certified). LUS emergency backstop via SST — sell-back path, not consumption. Eaton powers the facility. Delta powers the rack.

Cooling

C-001: Cooling Flow Schematic

River + Water Tower + CDU
Three-layer heat rejection. Layer 1 — Rack: Motivair MCDU-70 or Delta 140 kW CDU (NVL72 certified) connects rack coldplates to facility loop via Staubli UQD couplings (NVIDIA-approved). Layer 2 — Thermal buffer: existing on-site water tower absorbs load spikes, radiates overnight when ambient drops. Layer 3 — Primary free cooling: Vermilion River heat exchanger — river runs 65-75°F year-round vs 95°F Louisiana summer ambient; NVL72 accepts 45°C coolant inlet, river well within spec. Facility loop → plate heat exchanger → river. Replaces dry coolers during most operating hours. Munters desiccant humidity control — waste heat from NVL72 exhaust regenerates the desiccant wheel. Racks pay for their own dehumidification.
Power

P-001: Power Distribution Schematic

Full DC Microgrid
Full DC microgrid — no AC in the generation or distribution path. Bloom SOFC 10 MW (natural gas → DC direct, 54% efficiency, modular 65 kW units, 9% overbuild for 99.9% uptime). Solar collection layer: 1500V DC (NEC LVDC ceiling) → MPPT DC-DC → battery bank. 800V DC distribution bus (OCP Stage 1d) feeds all rack rows. Supercapacitors on bus handle AI training load spikes. Run:ai orchestrates Bloom throttle, solar dispatch, and CDU flow in real time — fully autonomous. Phase 1: 50 NVL72 racks, 6.5 MW IT load, 10 MW total facility. ABB SACE Infinitus DC protection. Delta 660 kW Power Rack at rack level. LUS emergency backstop via SST — grid is sell-back only.
Solar

L-001: Solar Rooftop Layout

2.05 MW | 3,731 Panels
First Solar Series 7 TR1 panels (550W, American-made, New Iberia LA — 30 miles) across Building 3 rooftop — Phase 1. 3,731 panels = 2.05 MW (First Solar engineered). 170,000 sq ft total rooftop across all 4 buildings. Collection layer: 1500V DC strings (NEC LVDC ceiling) — same power at lower current, I²R losses drop by the square vs lower-voltage strings, smaller wire gauge across 170K sq ft. MPPT DC-DC converter → battery bank → 800V DC bus. 97% solar-to-rack efficiency vs 92% AC path. No inverters, no AC conversion losses. Full 4-building rooftop engineering TBD — Phase 1 is Building 3.
