Ragin' Cajun Compute Campus — Engineering Blueprints

Trappeys Cannery, Lafayette, LA | 6-Sheet MVP Design Intent Package | NOT FOR CONSTRUCTION
Site

S-001: Site Plan

22 Acres | 112,500 SF
4 buildings with dimensions: Front (300x75, bayou view), Middle Low (300x100), Middle High (300x75, PHASE 1 DEPLOY), Rear High (250x150, staging). Water tower, concrete pad infrastructure yard, Bayou Vermilion frontage. Deploy sequence: Stage in Rear High, deploy compute in Middle High.
Electrical

E-001: Electrical Single-Line Diagram

800V DC Native
ATMOS gas to Caterpillar G3516J generators (480V AC). Eaton Beam Rubin DSX rectification (facility level) with ORV3 sidecar distribution to 800V DC bus. ABB SACE Infinitus solid-state DC breakers for arc-free protection. Delta 660 kW Power Rack (6x 110 kW shelves, 480 kW embedded BBU) with e-Fuse SiC switch (<3 μs fault cutoff) and 90 kW DC/DC shelf (800V to 50V, 98.5%, NVIDIA MGX) at rack level. Delta 140 kW In-Rack CDU (4RU, NVL72 certified). TI 800V-to-6V GaN + Infineon CoolGaN IBC for final power conversion. Solar DC-Direct bypass at 97% efficiency. LUS sell-back. Diesel emergency. BESS ride-through. Eaton powers the facility. Delta powers the rack.
Architectural

A-001: Compute Hall Floor Plan — Middle High

300' x 75' | Phase 1
Middle High building (22,500 sq ft) — Phase 1 compute hall. 4 NVL72 racks in hot/cold aisle containment. CDU area, electrical room, wooden truss structure preserved. Loading access from Rear High staging area. Cooling pipes from water tower.
Cooling

C-001: Cooling Flow Schematic

Water Tower + Dry Coolers
Water tower primary cooling loop (100 ft, thermal buffer). CoolIT CHx200 CDUs (200 kW each, sized for Phase 1) distribute to NVL72 rack manifolds via Staubli UQD couplings (NVIDIA-approved). BAC TrilliumSeries Adiabatic Coolers on concrete pad for supplemental heat rejection (44% energy reduction, adiabatic assist for 95F+ Lafayette summers). Munters HCD desiccant humidity control. 45C hot water supply, no mechanical chillers in mild weather.
Power

P-001: Power Distribution Schematic

4-Layer Hierarchy
Complete 4-layer hierarchy: Solar (rooftop 2.05 MW), Gas (Caterpillar G3516J generators, 1.6 MW each, N+1 = 3 units), Diesel (emergency), Grid (LUS sell-back only). Facility power: Eaton Beam Rubin DSX 800V DC rectification with ORV3 sidecar distribution (+/-400V, 800 kW/row). ABB SACE Infinitus DC protection. Eaton xStorage BESS for microgrid buffer and ride-through. Rack power: Delta 660 kW Power Rack (480 kW embedded BBU), e-Fuse (<3 μs), 90 kW DC/DC (800V to 50V, 98.5%), 140 kW In-Rack CDU (NVL72 certified). Phase 1: 650 kW total, gensets at 22% load with massive headroom.
Solar

L-001: Solar Rooftop Layout

2.05 MW | 3,731 Panels
First Solar Series 7 TR1 panels (550W, American-made New Iberia LA — 30 miles) across all 4 rooftops. Front: 658 panels, Middle Low: 1,105, Middle High: 658, Rear High: 1,310. 3,731 panels = 2.05 MW. 5-panel strings at 952V, buck to 800V DSX bus — 97% efficiency. No AC conversion losses.
