Ragin' Cajun Compute Campus — Engineering Blueprints

Trappeys Cannery, Lafayette, LA | 6-Sheet MVP Design Intent Package | NOT FOR CONSTRUCTION
Site

S-001: Site Plan

22 Acres | 112,500 SF
4 buildings with dimensions: Front (300x75, bayou view), Middle Low (300x100), Middle High (300x75, PHASE 1 DEPLOY), Rear High (250x150, staging). Water tower, concrete pad infrastructure yard, Bayou Vermilion frontage. Deploy sequence: Stage in Rear High, deploy compute in Middle High.
Electrical

E-001: Electrical Single-Line Diagram

800V DC Native
ATMOS gas to Cat G3520C generators (480V AC), Eaton Beam Rubin DSX rectification to 800V DC bus, busway to rack PDUs, 64:1 LLC converters to GPU. Solar DC-Direct bypass at 97% efficiency. LUS sell-back. Diesel emergency. BESS ride-through.
Architectural

A-001: Compute Hall Floor Plan — Middle High

300' x 75' | Phase 1
Middle High building (22,500 sq ft) — Phase 1 compute hall. 4 NVL72 racks in hot/cold aisle containment. CDU area, electrical room, wooden truss structure preserved. Loading access from Rear High staging area. Cooling pipes from water tower.
Cooling

C-001: Cooling Flow Schematic

Water Tower + Dry Coolers
Water tower (100 ft, thermal buffer) to dry coolers on concrete pad, through plate heat exchangers, CDU distribution to NVL72 rack manifolds. 45C hot water supply, no mechanical chillers in mild weather. Flow temperatures labeled throughout.
Power

P-001: Power Distribution Schematic

4-Layer Hierarchy
Complete 4-layer hierarchy: Solar (rooftop 2.05 MW), Gas (Cat G3520C backbone), Diesel (emergency), Grid (LUS sell-back only). Fuel-to-chip path through Eaton 800V DC platform. Phase 1: 650 kW total, gensets at 22% load with massive headroom.
Solar

L-001: Solar Rooftop Layout

2.05 MW | 3,731 Panels
First Solar Series 7 TR1 rooftop layout across all 4 buildings. Front: 658 panels, Middle Low: 1,105, Middle High: 658, Rear High: 1,310. Total: 3,731 panels = 2.05 MW. DC-Direct to 800V bus at 97% efficiency. Made in New Iberia, 30 miles away.
