Stage 1 operational layout: 44 cassettes across B3 (20) and B4 (24), 11 blocks of 4 cassettes each, 44 Cat CG260-16 gensets grouped by block on the rear slab, cooling tower field in the infrastructure yard, 2.05 MW First Solar rooftop solar on B1/B2, behind-the-meter permanent electrical island. Full Build (Stage 2) doubles to 88 cassettes / 22 blocks / 182.2 MW on the same site. Historic water tower and Vermilion River frontage are site amenities with no thermal role in the cooling plant.
