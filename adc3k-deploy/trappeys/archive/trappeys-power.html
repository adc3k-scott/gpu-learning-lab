Eaton Beam Rubin DSX — Five-Layer Power Architecture

Co-designed with NVIDIA. Presented live at NVIDIA GTC 2026. ADC deploys all five layers, with Bloom SOFC replacing the traditional AC genset path.

Layer 1 — Generation & Grid
Power Generation + Grid Connection
Bloom SOFC (DC direct, 10 MW Phase 1 → 100 MW) + First Solar 2.26 MW (1500V collection) + battery bank. LUS grid = sell-back only.
Layer 2 — Gray Space
MV Power Conditioning + Stability
Solid State Transformers — scalable, pad-mount or modular. Steps medium voltage to 800V DC (compute path) and 480V 3-phase (mechanical path). Switchgear, UPS, cable management.
Layer 3 — White Space
800V DC Delivery + Distribution
High-capacity busway distributes 800V DC across all rack rows. Plug-and-play rack feeds. Branch circuits with local protection and monitoring. Deployed overhead, minimizing raised floor complexity.
Layer 4 — Cooling
Power + Cooling for High-Density Racks
Coordinated liquid cooling for 130+ kW/rack. ADC: in-rack CDUs + Trappeys water tower + Vermilion River heat rejection. 45°C hot water loop certified for NVIDIA NVL72.
Layer 5 — Software & Services
Control, Health, Digital Twin
Eaton Brightlayer + Siemens Omnivise dispatch optimizer + NVIDIA Omniverse DSX digital twin. Real-time monitoring across all ADC sites. Predictive maintenance. Autonomous generation optimization.
