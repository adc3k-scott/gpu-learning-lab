Louisiana's AI Tax AdvantageBest incentive stack in the country for AI infrastructure

ITEP — Industrial Tax Exemption Program
Up to 80% property tax abatement for 10 years
Must file BEFORE groundbreaking. Non-negotiable. This is the single most time-sensitive item. Manufacturing/industrial operations qualify. ADC's AI compute facilities fit the definition.
HB 827 — Data Center Tax Incentive
Sales tax exemption on equipment
Louisiana's dedicated AI/data center incentive. Exempts qualifying equipment purchases from state and local sales tax. Phase 1 below $200M threshold — pursue parish-level PILOT as alternative or supplement.
Federal + State Historic Tax Credits
45% combined (Trappeys Cannery)
20% federal + 25% Louisiana state for qualified rehabilitation of historic structures. Trappeys Cannery qualifies. Requires NPS Part 1/2/3 application. Adaptive reuse of industrial heritage for AI infrastructure.
R&D Tax Credit
Louisiana R&D credit on qualifying activities
AI model development, inference optimization, drone autonomy R&D all qualify. Stackable with federal R&D credit.
Quality Jobs Program
Cash rebate on new payroll
Louisiana rebates a percentage of new payroll for qualifying jobs. AI facility positions exceed the average wage threshold. 75+ jobs across three facilities.
Enterprise Zone
Hiring credits in designated areas
Tax credits for hiring residents of Enterprise Zones. Multiple Lafayette-area zones may apply to ADC facility locations.
