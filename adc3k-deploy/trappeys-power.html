The 64:1 LLC Converter — What Happens Inside the Rack

You don't buy this. It ships inside the NVIDIA NVL72 rack. But here's what it does:

Input: 800V DC from the Eaton bus, through Delta 660 kW Power Rack + e-Fuse protection
Topology: LLC resonant converter (Direct Current Transformer)
Ratio: 64:1 step-down (800V → 12.5V)
Efficiency: Up to 98%
Location: Mounted directly adjacent to the GPU — shortest possible power path
Output: 12V DC to GPU voltage regulators, then core voltage (~0.7-1.0V) to the GPU die

Why LLC? Zero-voltage switching (ZVS) = minimal switching losses. At 64:1 ratio, it acts as a DC transformer — fixed ratio, no regulation needed. The upstream 800V bus handles voltage regulation. This lets the converter be tiny, efficient, and dead reliable.

Why this matters: Traditional racks have 8+ power supply units (PSUs) with fans taking up rack space. 800V DC eliminates all of them. More space for compute. Fewer fans = less noise, less heat, fewer failures. NVIDIA says 70% lower maintenance costs.

Eaton Beam Rubin DSX — Five-Layer Power Architecture

Co-designed with NVIDIA. Presented live at NVIDIA GTC 2026. ADC deploys all five layers, with Bloom SOFC DC generation replacing the traditional AC genset path.

Layer 1 — Generation & Grid
Power Generation + Grid Connection
Generation + BESS. ADC: Bloom SOFC 10 MW (DC direct) + First Solar 2.26 MW (1500V collection) + battery bank + LUS grid sell-back only.
Layer 2 — Gray Space
MV Power Conditioning + Stability
Solid State Transformers (SST) — scalable, pad-mount or modular. "Shovel to revenue" speed. Steps 15–35 kV to 800V DC (compute path) and 480V 3-phase (mechanical path). Switchgear, MVSST, UPS, cable management.
Layer 3 — White Space
800V DC Delivery + Distribution
Busway Distribution Center + high-capacity bus bars distribute 800V DC. Bus plugs provide plug-and-play rack feeds. Supports thousands of amps. Deployed overhead or underfloor. DC Remote Power Panel (DCRPP) sidecar splits 800V DC into branch circuits with local protection + monitoring.
Layer 4 — Cooling
Power + Cooling for High-Density Racks
Coordinated liquid cooling for 130+ kW/rack. ADC: Delta 140 kW in-rack CDU + BAC dry coolers + Trappeys water tower. 45°C hot water loop certified for NVIDIA NVL72.
Layer 5 — Software & Services
Control, Health, Digital Twin
Eaton Brightlayer EPMS + Siemens Omnivise dispatch optimizer + NVIDIA Omniverse DSX digital twin. Real-time monitoring across all ADC sites. Predictive maintenance. 15-min generation optimization loop.
