Water

600,000 Cubic Feet Per Second

Semiconductor fabs consume millions of gallons of ultra-pure water daily for wafer processing and cooling. Texas is rationing water. Louisiana has more than it knows what to do with.

  • Mississippi River: 600,000 cfs average flow. No allocation disputes. No drought restrictions. Ever.
  • Atchafalaya Basin: Second largest river swamp in the United States. Massive supplemental supply.
  • Industrial water treatment: Existing infrastructure along the entire River Road corridor — refineries, chemical plants, and LNG terminals already drawing and treating river water at scale.
  • Texas comparison: Colorado River drought restrictions, Edwards Aquifer pumping limits, active water wars with agriculture. TSMC Arizona is already fighting for water rights.
