Tax Incentive Stack

The Best Incentive Package in the Country for AI Infrastructure

Louisiana offers a layered incentive stack that no other state can match for AI factory development. Combined, these programs dramatically reduce effective capital costs and accelerate payback.

Program Benefit Details
ITEP 80% property tax abatement, 10 years Must file BEFORE groundbreaking. Pre-qualify with LED as "emerging industry" under NAICS code. Estimated savings: $8–16M.
Most time-sensitive item
Federal Historic Tax Credit 20% of qualified rehab expenditures Trappeys Cannery qualifies for the National Register. Applies to certified rehabilitation of historic structures.
Louisiana Historic Tax Credit 25% state credit Stacks with federal for 45% combined. Louisiana's state credit is among the most generous in the nation.
HB 827 Sales tax exemption on equipment Covers GPU racks, cooling infrastructure, power distribution, networking equipment.
R&D Tax Credit Qualified research expenditures AI model development, inference optimization, Dynamo performance tuning all qualify.
Quality Jobs Program Cash rebate on new payroll Direct cash rebate for each qualifying job created. Applies to engineering, operations, and technical staff.
Enterprise Zone Hiring credits Per-hire tax credits for qualifying employees. Stackable with Quality Jobs.
Act 730 20-year sales tax exemption Separate from HB 827. Requires $200M+ capital investment and 50+ jobs. Long-term operational savings exceeding $200M.
