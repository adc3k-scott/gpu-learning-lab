MARLIE I — Engineering Blueprints

Lafayette AI Factory & Command Center | 24x40 ft, 2 Floors, 8 NVL72 Racks, 576 GPUs | NOT FOR CONSTRUCTION
Site

S-001: Site Plan

0.60 Acres
24x40 ft building on 3 parcels (0.60 acres). Concrete pad for generators and dry coolers. 3 blighted structures for Phase 1 demolition. 0.5 miles from Trappeys. 0.5 miles from UL Lafayette. 60 miles from Willow Glen via dedicated fiber.
Electrical

E-001: Electrical Single-Line Diagram

800V DC Native
Caterpillar G3512H generators (1.03 MW each, N+1 = 2 units) through Eaton Beam Rubin DSX rectification (facility level) with ORV3 sidecar distribution to 800V DC bus. ABB SACE Infinitus solid-state DC breakers. Delta 660 kW Power Rack (6x 110 kW shelves, 480 kW embedded BBU) with e-Fuse SiC switch (<3 μs fault cutoff) and 90 kW DC/DC shelf (800V to 50V, 98.5%, NVIDIA MGX) at rack level. Delta 140 kW In-Rack CDU (4RU, NVL72 certified). TI 800V-to-6V GaN + Infineon CoolGaN IBC for final conversion. First Solar DC-Direct + Eaton xStorage BESS (600 kWh LFP, 761-1200 VDC). LUS grid backup. Diesel emergency. 8 NVL72 racks across 2 floors. Eaton powers the facility. Delta powers the rack.
Architectural

A-001: Floor Plans — System 1 & System 2

2 Floors | 4 Racks Each
Side-by-side floor plans. Downstairs (System 1): 4 NVL72 racks, hot/cold aisle, CDU pair, electrical panel. Upstairs (System 2): identical layout. Staircase shown. Building cross-section. Same footprint as a 40-ft shipping container.
Cooling

C-001: Cooling Flow Schematic

BAC Adiabatic + CoolIT CDU
BAC TrilliumSeries Adiabatic Coolers on concrete pad (44% energy reduction vs dry cooling, adiabatic assist for 95F+ Lafayette summers). Facility loop to 2 CoolIT CHx200 CDU pairs (200 kW each, 1 pair per floor). Staubli UQD quick-disconnect couplings (NVIDIA-approved, drip-free). Delta 140 kW In-Rack CDU (4RU, NVL72 certified) at each rack converts row-level coolant to chip-level cooling. Rack manifolds for all 8 NVL72 racks. 45C hot water supply, 55-65C return. Munters HCD desiccant dehumidifier for humidity control. Cooling chain: BAC Adiabatic (external) -> CoolIT CHx200 (row-level) -> Delta 140 kW (in-rack) -> NVIDIA manifolds (chip-level).
Power

P-001: Power Distribution Schematic

4-Layer Hierarchy
4-layer hierarchy — DIFFERENT from other sites: Solar (offset) + Gas (backbone, Cat G3512H) + Grid (LUS backup) + Diesel (emergency). Grid is backup here, not sell-back. Facility power: Eaton Beam Rubin DSX 800V DC rectification with ORV3 sidecar distribution (+/-400V, 800 kW/row). ABB SACE Infinitus DC protection. Eaton xStorage BESS (761-1200 VDC) for microgrid islanding. Rack power: Delta 660 kW Power Rack (480 kW embedded BBU), e-Fuse (<3 μs), 90 kW DC/DC (800V to 50V, 98.5%), 140 kW In-Rack CDU (NVL72 certified).
Solar

L-001: Solar & Battery Layout

300 kW + 600 kWh BESS
960 sq ft roof layout with First Solar Series 7 TR1 panels (550W, American-made Ohio/New Iberia LA). 5-panel strings at 952V, buck to 800V DSX bus — 97% path efficiency. Ground-mount supplement on adjacent parcels. Eaton xStorage BESS (600 kWh LFP, 761-1200 VDC) for ATS bridge and peak shaving. DC-Direct to 800V bus via TI GaN converters.
