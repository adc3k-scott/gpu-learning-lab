How It Works

Access Panel Design

Work from outside, not inside. Every rack is accessible through top-hinged steel panels on both long walls.

8 panels total -- 4 per side, spanning the full rack zone. Each panel approximately 8 ft wide by 7.5 ft tall.

Top-hinged with gas strut prop rods. Open upward for weather protection while working. Cam-lock fasteners for transport security.

32 ft of panels covering all 10 rack positions. 8 ft of structural steel at each end (entry/CDU zones) maintains container integrity.

25.3 inches of reach from panel opening to rack face. Technician stands outside on grade -- no confined space entry required for routine service.

EPDM gasket sealed and bolted for weathertight transport. 14-gauge steel frame. Powder-coated exterior. Marine-grade hardware.

Configuration

What's Inside

A complete DGX SuperPOD with networking, storage, power, cooling, and safety systems -- all in 40 feet.

8 Compute Racks (R1-R8)

1 DGX SuperPOD = 576 GPUs. Each NVL72 rack: 72 Blackwell GPUs + 36 Grace CPUs. 130 kW per rack, direct-to-chip liquid cooled.

1 Networking Rack (R9)

Quantum-X800 InfiniBand spine switches. Ethernet ToR (25/100 GbE). Patch panels. Out-of-band management. Firewall/router.

1 Storage + Management Rack (R10)

NVMe-oF high-speed shared storage. NVIDIA Base Command Manager. Prometheus/Grafana monitoring. Jetson Orin edge controller. Rack-mount UPS.

Eaton Beam Rubin DSX — Facility Power

480V AC to 800V DC rectification. ORV3 Sidecar distribution (+/-400V, 800 kW/row). Overhead busway from electrical panel to each rack position. ABB SACE Infinitus solid-state DC breakers. 97% efficiency (vs 92% AC path).

Delta Electronics — Rack Power

660 kW Power Rack (6x 110 kW shelves, 480 kW embedded BBU — no separate UPS). Delta e-Fuse SiC switch (<3 μs fault cutoff, arc-flash prevention). 90 kW DC/DC Shelf (800V to 50V, 98.5%, NVIDIA MGX). 140 kW In-Rack CDU (4RU, NVL72 certified). Eaton powers the facility. Delta powers the rack.

Dual CDU System

Primary + redundant 250 kW CDUs. Quick-disconnect bulkhead fittings to external dry cooler. 45C supply / 55-65C return. Leak detection throughout.

Overhead Cable Trays

Unistrut-mounted perforated trays. Power on left, data on right (NEC 300.3). 18.2 inches clearance above racks. Drop-down to each rack position.

LED Lighting

Low-profile LED strips between cable tray runs. Adequate illumination for service access through panels. Minimal heat contribution.

Fire Suppression

Novec 1230 clean agent with ceiling-mounted nozzles. VESDA aspirating smoke detection (4 sampling points). Emergency disconnect at door entry.

Environmental Engineering

Engineered for Louisiana -- Built for Anywhere

Most companies design for a server room. We design for 95 F and 90% humidity.

❄

Liquid Cooling Loop

CDU inside connects to external dry cooler via quick-disconnect. 45 C supply, 55-60 C return. Adiabatic assist for peak Louisiana summer. Near water? Water-to-water heat exchanger option. All GPU heat exits the container -- zero heat buildup inside.

💧

Humidity Control

Desiccant dehumidifier strips moisture without cooling. Positive pressure blower keeps humid air out. Sealed container when panels closed. No air conditioning needed -- ASHRAE A1 rated. Control humidity, not temperature. This is the engineering nobody else does because they've never built in the Gulf.

☀

Solar Roof

320 sq ft of First Solar panels on the container roof. Dual purpose: shade blocks direct sun (reduces internal temp 10-15 F), power runs all internal support systems (dehumidifier, lights, monitoring, blower). Peak solar coincides with peak heat. DC-direct into the 800V bus. The sun works for you, not against you.
