Execution

Humidity + Airflow — Lafayette Reality

Lafayette averages 75-80% relative humidity. ASHRAE recommends 40-60% for compute. NVL72 racks have internal fans for power supplies, NVSwitches, and networking — they need dry, moving air. This is not optional.

DEHUMIDIFICATION

Desiccant System

  • Industrial desiccant dehumidifiers (no refrigerant)
  • Target: 40-50% RH inside warehouse
  • Solar-powered during daytime (energy-efficient)
  • Prevents condensation on cold surfaces
  • Prevents corrosion on electronics + connectors
  • Drop ceiling or containment zone optional
AIRFLOW

HVLS Fans + Circulation

  • HVLS (High Volume Low Speed) ceiling fans
  • Big Ass Fans or equivalent — 20-24 ft diameter
  • Move air throughout 25-30 ft warehouse ceiling
  • Prevent moisture stratification
  • Assist rack internal fans (dry ambient air intake)
  • Very low power draw (~1-2 kW per fan)
ELEVATED RACKS

Underfloor Airflow

  • NVL72 racks on raised pedestals/stands
  • 12-18 inches above concrete floor
  • Air flows underneath — assists rack intake fans
  • Front-to-back airflow path (cold aisle / warm aisle)
  • Concrete floor is 100+ years strong — handles the weight
  • Moisture doesn't pool under racks
  • Cable routing underneath

NVL72 racks have internal fans. The liquid cooling handles GPU heat (cold plates + 45°C hot water). But the power supplies, NVSwitches, networking gear, and motherboard components still use internal fans for airflow. Those fans pull in ambient air — if that air is 80% humidity, you're blowing moisture over hot electronics. Dehumidification is mandatory for a first-class setup in Louisiana. Not refrigerant, not air conditioning — just dry air and good circulation. Solar panels can power the dehumidifiers during the day.
