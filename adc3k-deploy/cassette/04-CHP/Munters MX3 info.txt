Munters launches new MX3 dehumidifier series
Mar 3, 2026
Munters, the global leader in energy-efficient air treatment solutions, is launching an all-new series of high-performance dehumidifiers, the MX3 and MX3 PLUS. Offering up to 20% lower energy consumption, the series is designed to meet the most demanding industrial and commercial climate control requirements.
Building on decades of desiccant dehumidification expertise, the Munters MX3 Series is a a powerful, scalable platform for full humidity and temperature control.
Whether deployed as a standalone powerhouse (Munters MX3) or integrated into a highly configurable air treatment system (Munters MX3 PLUS), this new product family offers flexibility and intelligent operation for critical applications such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, storage and preservation.
“MX3 is engineered for professional customers who demand high capacity, absolute precision, reliability, and sustainability,” says Camilla Engbrink, VP R&D and Offer Management, AirTech at Munters. “With Munters MX3 and MX3 PLUS, we’ve applied advanced purge technology to deliver our most energy-efficient, customizable dehumidification solution yet.”
The MX3 dehumidifiers come with various airflow capacities in different sizes. The MX3 can also be integrated as the centerpiece of a larger customized MX3 PLUS system, equipped with pre– and/or post-treatment modules. Features include:

* 15-20% lower energy consumption compared to previous models, and up to 15% reduction in CO2 emissions
* Optional AirC Connect for remote monitoring and control

* Scalable and fully customizable configurations
* High-capacity moisture removal
Powerful, scalable and smart

* Airflows from 3,000 to 9,500 m³/h
* Fully customizable configurations
* Options for gas, steam, or electric reactivation
* Pre– and/or post-treatment modules for filtering, heating, cooling and humidification available for the MX3 PLUS systems
* Munters AirPro enclosure for enhanced flexibility and reliable, consistent performance
The MX3 Series is now available on selected markets. For more information, visit: https://www.munters.com/en-gb/campaign-pages/mx3-series-engineered-for-climate-excellence/
