05Thermal Engineering

Design Philosophy

No air conditioning. No human occupancy. Control humidity, not temperature. Solar roof for shade. Desiccant for moisture. Exhaust fan for convection. 100% AI-monitored.

ParameterValue
Max interior ambient45°C (113°F)
Coolant supply45°C (113°F)
Coolant return55-65°C (131-149°F)
Flow rate per rack80-130 LPM (21-34 GPM)
System flow (10 racks)1,300 LPM (343 GPM)
Liquid-cooled portion87% (~1,131 kW)
Air-cooled portion13% (~169 kW)

Solar roof reduces interior temps 10-25°F vs unshaded container. All temps within ASHRAE A4 envelope (45°C max). No refrigerant, no compressor, no maintenance.

What We Do NOT Need

No air conditioning. No raised floor. No chilled water. No human-comfort HVAC. No overhead cable trays. The liquid cooling loop handles 87% of heat directly. Ambient air management handles the remaining 13%.

Eaton Beam Rubin DSX — Five-Layer Power Architecture

Presented live at NVIDIA GTC 2026 by JP Buzzell, VP & Chief Architect, Eaton Global Data Center Segment.

Layer 1 — Generation & Grid
Power Generation + Grid
Bloom SOFC (natural gas → 800V DC direct, 54% efficiency) + First Solar solar supplement + diesel emergency backup.
Layer 2 — Gray Space
MV Conditioning + Stability
Solid State Transformers (SST). 15–35 kV → 800V DC (compute) + 480V 3-phase (mechanical).
Layer 3 — White Space
800V DC Delivery
Busway Distribution Center + busbars + DCRPP sidecar. Plug-and-play rack feeds.
Layer 4 — Cooling
High-Density Rack Cooling
Delta 140 kW CDU. 45°C hot water loop. 130+ kW per rack supported.
Layer 5 — Software
Control + Digital Twin
Eaton Brightlayer + Siemens Omnivise + NVIDIA Omniverse DSX twin.
