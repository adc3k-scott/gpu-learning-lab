05Thermal Engineering

Design Philosophy

No air conditioning. No human occupancy. Control humidity, not temperature. Solar roof for shade. Desiccant for moisture. Exhaust fan for convection. 100% AI-monitored.

ParameterValue
Max interior ambient45°C (113°F)
Coolant supply45°C (113°F)
Coolant return55-65°C (131-149°F)
Flow rate per rack80-130 LPM (21-34 GPM)
System flow (10 racks)1,300 LPM (343 GPM)
Liquid-cooled portion87% (~1,131 kW)
Air-cooled portion13% (~169 kW)
Exhaust fans2x 2,500 CFM roof-mount

Louisiana Climate Performance

ConditionOutsidePod Interior
Winter avg55°F60°F
Spring / Fall80°F90°F
Summer avg92°F105°F
Summer peak100°F115°F

Solar roof reduces interior temps 10-25°F vs unshaded container. All temps within ASHRAE A4 envelope (45°C max). No refrigerant, no compressor, no maintenance.

What We Do NOT Need

No air conditioning. No raised floor. No chilled water. No human-comfort HVAC. No overhead cable trays. The liquid cooling loop handles 87% of heat directly. Ambient air management handles the remaining 13%.
