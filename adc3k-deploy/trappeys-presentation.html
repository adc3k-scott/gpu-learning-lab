4,000 Years on One Piece of Land

~2000 BCAtakapa-Ishak people — the Ciwat (Alligator Band) — inhabit the Vermilion River. Continuous occupation for 4,000 years.
1920sTexaco leases the property to Baldwin Lumber & Star Salt Company.
1927The Great Flood. 10 feet of water. Buildings damaged but survive.
1930B.F. Trappey's Sons opens the cannery. Builds its own electric powerhouse.
1941-45WWII — 12 million lbs of candied yams for Allied forces. 350-600 workers.
1991Plant closes. Sits empty for 30+ years.
2026ADC brings it back as Louisiana's first AI factory.
