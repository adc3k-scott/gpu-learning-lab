4,000 Years on One Piece of Land

~2000 BCAtakapa-Ishak people — the Ciwat (Alligator Band) — live along the Vermilion River.
1920sTexaco leases to Baldwin Lumber & Star Salt.
1927Great Flood — 10 feet of water. Buildings damaged but survive.
1930Trappey's opens. Builds its own electric powerhouse. 350-600 workers.
1941-45WWII — 12 million lbs of candied yams for Allied troops.
1991Plant closes. Sits empty for 30+ years.
2026ADC restores it as mission critical AI infrastructure for all of Lafayette.
