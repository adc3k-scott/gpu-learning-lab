Tiger Compute Campus — Engineering Blueprints

Willow Glen Terminal, St. Gabriel, LA | MVP Design Intent Package | NOT FOR CONSTRUCTION
Electrical

E-001: Electrical Single-Line Diagram

800V DC Native
Complete power path from Entergy 500kV/230kV substations through Cat CG260 generators, Eaton Beam Rubin DSX rectification, 800V DC distribution bus, to NVIDIA NVL72 GPU racks. First Solar DC-direct bypass at 97% efficiency. 4-layer power hierarchy: Solar → Gas → Diesel → Grid (sell-back only).
Cooling

C-001: Cooling Flow Schematic

Mississippi River Once-Through
Complete cooling path from Mississippi River intake through plate heat exchangers, facility cooling loop, CDUs, to liquid-cooled NVL72 rack manifolds. 45C hot water supply — no chillers needed. Future waste heat recovery: ORC (8-11 MW electricity) + absorption chillers (11,000+ tons). LPDES permit LA0005851 already on-site.
Site

S-001: Site Plan

700 Acres
Full 700-acre property layout. Compute hall, generator yard, power building, two solar array zones (5+ MW), existing 500kV and 230kV substations, tank farm (WGT operations), deepwater dock, water intake structure, CN Railroad, gas pipeline corridor, access road from LA-75. Mississippi River on west edge with 3,500 ft frontage.
Architectural

A-001: Compute Hall Floor Plan

36 NVL72 Racks
Phase 1 compute hall: 200' x 100' (20,000 sq ft). 36 NVIDIA NVL72 racks in 6 rows, hot aisle containment, cold aisle supply. Mechanical room with CDUs (N+1). NOC, loading dock, electrical room with 800V DC busway. 2,592 GPUs total. 4.68 MW IT load. Designed for expansion to 72 racks.
Power

P-001: Power Distribution Schematic

4-Layer Hierarchy
Fuel-to-chip power distribution with complete 4-layer hierarchy. ATMOS gas pipeline to Cat CG260 generators, through paralleling switchgear, Eaton Beam Rubin DSX rectification, 800V DC bus, busway to rack PDUs, 64:1 LLC converters to GPU. First Solar DC-Direct bypass. BESS ride-through. Grid sell-back only. Full Layer 1-4 breakdown with specs.
Solar

L-001: Solar Array Layout

First Solar DC-Direct
First Solar Series 7 TR1 ground-mount array. 18 rows, ~4,500 panels, ~2.5 MW Zone A. 5-panel strings at 952V, buck-converted to 800V for direct feed to DSX bus — 97% path efficiency vs 88-92% traditional AC. Combiner boxes, DC-DC converters, string math, panel specs. Made in New Iberia, LA.
