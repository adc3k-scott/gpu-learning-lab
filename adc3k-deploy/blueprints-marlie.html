MARLIE I — Engineering Blueprints

Lafayette AI Factory & Command Center | 24x40 ft, 2 Floors, 8 NVL72 Racks, 576 GPUs | NOT FOR CONSTRUCTION
Site

S-001: Site Plan

0.60 Acres
24x40 ft building on 3 parcels (0.60 acres). Concrete pad for generators and dry coolers. 3 blighted structures for Phase 1 demolition. 0.5 miles from Trappeys. 0.5 miles from UL Lafayette. 60 miles from Willow Glen via dedicated fiber.
Electrical

E-001: Electrical Single-Line Diagram

800V DC Native
Cat G3520C generators (1.5 MW, N+1) through Eaton Beam Rubin DSX to 800V DC bus. Solar DC-Direct + 600 kWh battery. LUS grid backup. Diesel emergency. 8 NVL72 racks across 2 floors.
Architectural

A-001: Floor Plans — System 1 & System 2

2 Floors | 4 Racks Each
Side-by-side floor plans. Downstairs (System 1): 4 NVL72 racks, hot/cold aisle, CDU pair, electrical panel. Upstairs (System 2): identical layout. Staircase shown. Building cross-section. Same footprint as a 40-ft shipping container.
Cooling

C-001: Cooling Flow Schematic

Dry Coolers
Dry coolers on concrete pad (no water tower, no river). Facility loop to 2 CDU pairs (1 per floor). Rack manifolds for all 8 NVL72 racks. 45C hot water supply. Temperature labels throughout.
Power

P-001: Power Distribution Schematic

4-Layer Hierarchy
4-layer hierarchy — DIFFERENT from other sites: Solar (offset) + Gas (backbone) + Grid (LUS backup) + Diesel (emergency). Grid is backup here, not sell-back. Full fuel-to-chip path through Eaton 800V DC.
Solar

L-001: Solar & Battery Layout

300 kW + 600 kWh BESS
960 sq ft roof layout with First Solar panels. Ground-mount supplement on adjacent parcels. 600 kWh LFP battery for ATS bridge and peak shaving. DC-Direct to 800V bus.
