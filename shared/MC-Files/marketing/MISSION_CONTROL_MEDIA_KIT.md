# MISSION CONTROL — MEDIA KIT

## One-Liner
Free AI-powered vehicle diagnostic platform for Harley riders, car owners, truckers, e-bike riders, and scooter owners.

## 100-Word Boilerplate
Mission Control provides 41 free diagnostic tools and AI-powered chat across five vehicle platforms: Harley-Davidson, Automotive, Trucking, E-Bike, and Scooter. Built by a solo developer in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, the platform helps vehicle owners diagnose issues, track maintenance, and connect with a community. The AI uses platform-specific expertise for relevant guidance. Free tools include DTC databases, flowcharts, calculators, and reference guides. Premium and Pro tiers add expanded AI access, morning briefs, and advanced features.
