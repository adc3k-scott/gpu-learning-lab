About This Service

What Is This? Who Runs It? Is It Free?

Louisiana's AI Infrastructure Initiative is a free public service.

We built this system to accelerate the process of getting Louisiana's schools, universities, and communities connected to AI compute infrastructure. The federal and state governments have allocated billions of dollars in funding for exactly this purpose. The problem is nobody is doing the paperwork. Programs expire. Money goes unclaimed. Louisiana falls behind.

This platform is powered by AI agents that help identify which programs your institution qualifies for, pre-fill application templates, and track deadlines. It's designed so that anyone — a teacher, a principal, a university administrator, a city official, or a concerned citizen — can sit down, register their institution, and start the process.

There is no fee. There is no charge. There will never be a charge. We do not sell your information. We do not share your information with third parties unless you specifically direct us to file an application on your behalf. This is a service for Louisiana, by Louisiana.

What will you do with my information?
We use your information solely to identify which federal, state, and local programs your institution qualifies for. We do not sell it, share it with advertisers, or give it to anyone else. Your data is stored securely and encrypted. The only time we share your information externally is when we file a grant application on your behalf — and only with your explicit approval.
Is this really free?
Yes. 100% free. We will never ask for a credit card, a payment, or any financial information. This is a public service. We will never charge schools or users. Registration and eligibility assessment are completely free.
Who runs this?
Louisiana's AI Infrastructure Initiative is operated out of Lafayette, Louisiana. The system is powered by AI agents that specialize in grant eligibility, filing processes, and deadline tracking. Human oversight is maintained on all applications — no paperwork is filed without your review and approval.
Is this a government program?
No. This is a private initiative that helps institutions access existing government programs. We do not represent any government agency. We are a service that connects schools to programs that already exist but are underutilized because nobody is doing the paperwork.
Can I use this to help someone else register?
Absolutely. Anyone can use this system to register an institution. A teacher can register their school. A parent can register their child's school. A city official can register multiple schools in their district. The more people who participate, the faster Louisiana catches up.
How is my data protected?
All form submissions are transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit). Data is stored on secure servers. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. We do not sell data. We do not partner with data brokers. Your information exists for one purpose: to help your institution access the funding it's entitled to.
What happens after I register?
Within 24 hours, you'll receive an eligibility report showing which programs your institution qualifies for. From there, we help prepare and file applications. You review and approve everything before it's submitted. Nothing happens without your knowledge and consent.
