| System | Specs | Status |
|---|---|---|

### 4D. Strategic Plan 2025-2030

- Expansion of AI workload capabilities
- Modernization of the fiber backbone
- Increased GPU compute resources
- Broader PaaS (Platform as a Service) offerings to member institutions

### 5C. LONI Integration Opportunity

LONI's 1,600-mile fiber backbone with 40 Gbps and QB4's 144 A100 GPUs represent the state's existing HPC infrastructure. ADC's Trappeys facility with NVL72 racks would represent a massive upgrade to Louisiana's compute capacity. A partnership with LONI/Board of Regents could:
- Position ADC as the state's GPU compute backbone
- Enable all 8 primary LONI member universities to access ADC compute
- Align with LONI's 2025-2030 strategic plan to expand AI capabilities
- Leverage the existing 1,600-mile fiber network for connectivity

### 5D. 50-State Plan Alignment

ADC + UL Lafayette = Louisiana's version of what UF did in Florida ($180M, HiPerGator, K-12 pipeline, all 12 universities). The database above shows the scale: 45 institutions, 327,000 students, 69 K-12 districts, and an existing LONI backbone ready for a GPU compute upgrade.
