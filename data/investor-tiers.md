# Investor Package Tiers — Manual Review Before Sending

**Every registration at /alumni-investors goes to investors@louisianaai.net. Scott reviews and assigns a tier before any financial information is shared.**

---

## TIER 1 — General Interest / Alumni / Curious
**Who:** Alumni wanting to help, community members, teachers, local business owners, anyone who doesn't clearly identify as an investor
**What they get:**
- Thank you email
- Link to public pages (louisianaai.net, campus pages, blueprints, renders)
- Invitation to volunteer or spread the word
- Newsletter signup (when we build one)
- **NO financial data. NO projections. NO CapEx. NO token pricing.**

**Response template:** "Thank you for your interest in Louisiana's AI Infrastructure Initiative. Here's how you can get involved: [public links]. If you'd like to help spread the word to schools in your area, we'd appreciate it. We'll keep you updated as the project progresses."

---

## TIER 2 — Serious Individual Investor ($25K-$250K range)
**Who:** Individual investors, small business owners with capital, angel investors, people who specifically mention investing or ask about returns
**What they get:**
- Everything in Tier 1
- 2-page executive summary (high level — sites, technology, incentive stack overview)
- Invitation to a 30-minute discovery call with Scott
- **General incentive information but NO specific CapEx, revenue projections, or token pricing**

**Response template:** "Thank you for your interest in investing in Louisiana's AI infrastructure. I'd like to schedule a brief call to learn more about your investment goals and share an overview of what we're building. Are you available [suggest 2-3 times]?"

---

## TIER 3 — Qualified Investor ($250K-$5M range)
**Who:** Family offices, high-net-worth individuals, small funds, people with verified investment history, anyone who passes the discovery call
**What they get:**
- Everything in Tier 1 + 2
- Full financial package (both sites — CapEx, revenue projections, phase structure)
- Token economics brief
- Incentive stack analysis with dollar amounts
- Risk factors document
- Competitive landscape analysis
- Invitation to site visit
- **Requires signed NDA before full numbers are shared**

**Response template:** "Following up on our call — I'm sending the full financial package for both campus sites. This includes projections, incentive analysis, and risk factors. Please review the attached NDA — once signed, I'll send the complete package within 24 hours."

---

## TIER 4 — Institutional / Strategic ($5M+)
**Who:** Institutional funds, PE firms, strategic partners (NVIDIA, Eaton, etc.), government economic development agencies, anyone talking $5M+
**What they get:**
- Everything in Tier 1-3
- Custom presentation deck (audience-specific)
- Direct access to Scott
- Site visit + technical walkthrough
- Engineering package deep dive
- Partnership structure discussion
- Board/advisory seat discussion
- **Requires NDA + accredited investor verification**

**Response template:** Direct phone call from Scott. No template — this is personal.

---

## RED FLAGS — DO NOT SEND FINANCIAL INFO IF:
- They won't provide a real name or organization
- Email is a throwaway (gmail with random numbers, etc.)
- They ask for "all your financials" without context
- They mention competitors by name and ask for comparison
- They're media pretending to be investors
- Anything feels off — trust your gut

## WORKFLOW

1. Registration comes in at investors@louisianaai.net
2. Scott reviews: who are they, what org, what type, what interest
3. Scott assigns tier (1-4)
4. Appropriate response sent based on tier
5. Log the interaction in contacts-database.md
6. If Tier 3+, schedule call → send NDA → send package after signed
7. If red flag, respond politely and file in declined/
