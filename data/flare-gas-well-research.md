# Flare Gas Well Research — Lafayette-Area Parishes
Last updated: 2026-03-26

## Executive Summary

Research target: Active flare/gas well sites in **Vermilion, Iberia, St. Martin, and Acadia** parishes (all within 30-40 miles of Lafayette, LA) for deploying containerized AI compute at flare sites — converting waste gas to GPU-hours.

**Key finding:** These 4 parishes have **~400+ active producing wells** with combined gas production of **~1.0 Bcf/year** (Dec 2024 annualized). The biggest opportunity is concentrated gas production from a handful of major operators. Parish-level flare-specific volumes require SONRIS direct queries (see Action Items).

## Louisiana Flaring Context

- **27 Bcf** of methane wasted from Louisiana's 31,000 active onshore wells (EDF, 2019 data)
- **$82M** in natural gas wasted statewide (Louisiana Illuminator, 2023)
- **5.18 Bcf** specifically from venting and flaring = ~$16M lost (2019)
- **4,785 orphan wells** statewide as of April 2024, $542.9M to plug all
- **28,000+ wells** idling under "future utility" — $2.5B cleanup liability
- Flaring = ~20% of all methane wasted in Louisiana
- Small operators = 6% of production but 50% of well-site emissions
- **New regulation proposed:** Louisiana DNR/C&E working to prohibit routine venting and flaring (modeled on Colorado/New Mexico rules)

## Flare-to-Compute Business Model Reference

**Crusoe Energy (competitor/model):**
- Founded 2018. Digital Flare Mitigation (DFM) patent.
- Modules sized 70,000 - 300,000+ cfpd (cubic feet per day), deployable in series
- Signs gas purchase agreements with operators
- Claims 98% methane reduction, 63% CO2-equivalent reduction
- 40 US flare-powered facilities
- Pivoted to mega-campuses (Abilene TX, 1.2 GW for Oracle/Stargate)
- Electricity from flare gas: $0.02-0.05/kWh

**ADC Advantage over Crusoe for Louisiana:**
- Crusoe operates in Permian, Bakken, DJ Basin, Powder River, Uinta — NOT in Louisiana
- ADC is local (Lafayette) with existing infrastructure pipeline
- Henry Hub proximity = if gas ISN'T being flared, it's cheap pipeline gas
- Louisiana's regulatory shift (anti-flaring rules) creates urgency for operators to find alternatives
- ADC offers the alternative: we take your waste gas, you get compliance + revenue

## Compute Sizing — Gas Volume to GPU-Hours

| Gas Volume | Power Output (recip engine) | GPU Equivalent |
|------------|----------------------------|----------------|
| 70,000 cfpd (Crusoe minimum) | ~200 kW | ~2 NVL72 racks |
| 300,000 cfpd | ~860 kW | ~8-10 NVL72 racks |
| 1,000,000 cfpd (1 MMcfpd) | ~2.9 MW | ~1 ADC 3K pod |
| 10,000,000 cfpd (10 MMcfpd) | ~29 MW | ~10 ADC 3K pods |

**Target parishes produce ~32 MMcfpd combined** (966k MCF/mo / 30 days). If even 10% is being flared = ~3.2 MMcfpd = ~9 MW = ~3 ADC 3K pods worth of stranded energy.
